Runaway Girl,2014-2019

逃婚女孩,  2014-2019

在中国四川省凉山彝族自治州最核心的彝族聚居区，当地村民仍然保留着传统的生产生活方式。因为经济落后，这里的孩子一般在小 学毕业钱就辍学到东南沿海打工养家，她们的婚姻也由父母做主，甚至在未成年时期就被家人安排结婚。
打工的经历让这些女孩子接触到了当代的生活方式，爱情和婚姻观念发生了变化，因此与古老的家庭传统产生了冲突，她们中有一部 分人为了逃避家中安排的婚姻在外打工不愿回家。

Runaway Girl,  2014-2019

In the core Yi settlement area of Daliangshan Yi Autonomous Prefecture, Sichuan Province,China, local villagers still retain their traditional way of production and life. Due to thebackward economy, children here usually drop out of school after primary school and go to the southeast coast to work and support their families. Their marriage is also decided by their parents, and even arranged by their families when they are underage.
The experience of working has exposed these girls to contemporary lifestyles, changing ideas about love and marriage, and thus conflicts with ancient family traditions. Some of them do not want to go home in order to escape the arranged.
