Rural People,2012-2017

农村人,  2012-2017

该项目是2012年由摄影师焦冬子发起了，为西部农村人拍摄肖像照片的公益项目，总共有二十多位摄影师志愿参与，在青海、内蒙、 贵州、四川、宁夏等省份的上百个村庄，为村民拍摄了上万张照片并送给被摄者。该项目被多家媒体报道并获得首届公益慈善大奖， 并在举办了多次展览。

Rural People,  2012-2017

The project is a public welfare project initiated by photographer Jiao Dongzi in 2012 to take portraits of rural people in western China. A total of more than 20 photographers have volunteered to participate in the project, taking tens of thousands of photos for villagers in hundreds of villages in Qinghai, Inner Mongolia, Guizhou, Sichuan, Ningxia and other provinces and sending them to the subjects. The project was reported by many media and won the first public welfare and charity award, and held many exhibitions.
